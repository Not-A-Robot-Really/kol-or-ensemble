About the ensemble

Music shaped to carry clarity, warmth, and hope.

"The Sound of Light that Illuminates the World"

We are all freshmen from Needham High School who are performing in places to spread the beauty of music and help people know how it can touch hearts.

Kol Or Ensemble takes its name from the Hebrew words Kol, meaning sound, and Or, meaning light. Inspired by the idea that music can illuminate hearts and connect the world, the ensemble seeks to bring clarity, warmth, and hope through chamber music.

Each performance is an invitation to listen deeply and to experience sound as light.
